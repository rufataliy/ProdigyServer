Create shared events with your students

The integrated calendar enables creating simple, as well as, recurring events that could be synchronized between multiple users. By adding students to the event, we call it classes, you can share the event with your students without authorizing them to modify it.

If you want to share your content with your student it is as simple as creating programs and assigning them to the classes.

Built-in realtime chat

Communication is a key factor in the learning process. By keeping discussions with your students on the same platform prevents you to jump back and forth between applications and enables you to notify classes by sending group messages.
